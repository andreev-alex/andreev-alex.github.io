Insurance Policy Management Interface

Qatar Insurance is a major insurer with 1.1M+ clients and a $10B+ market cap. This project aimed to simplify the often complex process of managing insurance policies, offering users a cohesive platform to handle their insurance needs effectively. As a Lead Product Designer, I guided the creation of an intuitive web interface aimed at enhancing user experience and enabling smooth interaction with policy-related tasks.

Explore feature on qic.online
Business & User’s problem

Operational Cost Savings & Inconvenient Branch Visits

Customers face inconvenience and inefficiency as they are required to visit an offline branch for essential tasks such as renewing their policies, making installment payments, or upgrading their policy plans. This process is time-consuming and often disrupts their daily routines.

Task & Individual Contribution

Design an interface that allows users to perform these functions

  • Explore current policy status and information
  • Renew policy
  • Make an installment payment
  • Upgrade policy plan from TPL to Comprehensive
  • Download policy documents
