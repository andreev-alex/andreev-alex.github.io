Insurance Policy Management Interface

This project aimed to simplify the often complex process of managing insurance policies, offering users a cohesive platform to handle their insurance needs effectively. As a Lead Product Designer, I spearheaded the creation of an intuitive and efficient interface for managing insurance policies, enhancing user experience and facilitating seamless interactions with policy-related tasks.

Key Features

Policy Switching

Users have the freedom to switch to different policies directly through the interface, empowering them to adapt their insurance coverage to changing needs with ease and efficiency

Key Features

Flexible Payment Options

Users can conveniently pay for their insurance policy in installments, providing them with financial flexibility and easing the burden of lump-sum payments

Key Features

Personal Information Display

The interface displays pertinent personal information related to the policy, offering users clear visibility into their coverage details and facilitating informed decision-making

Key Features

Document Accessibility

The interface enables users to effortlessly download policy documents, ensuring easy access to essential information and enhancing transparency in policy details
