Driving Digital Transformation at Qatar Insurance Group (QIC)

Insurance Policy Management

As a Senior Product Designer at Qatar Insurance Group, I led the design of the Insurance Policy Management interface, transforming how over 1.1 million users manage their insurance policies online. This initiative played a pivotal role in QIC’s digital transformation, addressing key user pain points and business challenges.

About Qatar Insurance Group (QIC)

Founded in 1964, QIC is one of the Middle East’s leading insurance providers, with:

  • 60 years of industry experience
  • $1.74 billion in revenue (2024)
  • 1.1M+ customers

Users Problem

QIC customers faced challenges with the traditional process of managing insurance policies, which required visiting offline branches in Doha. This approach, while reliable, sometimes resulted in longer wait times, prompting the need for a more convenient digital alternative.

Solution

I designed a seamless, user-friendly interface enabling users to manage their policies entirely online. Key functionalities included:

  • Policy Renewal
  • Installment Payments
  • Plan Upgrades
  • Document Downloads
  • Current Policy Overview

Design Process

My iterative design process spanned 20 days, ensuring a robust, user-centric solution:

Week 1: Discovery & Analysis

  • Competitive Analysis
  • Information Architecture (IA)

Week 2: Wireframing & Feedback

  • User Flow Creation
  • Wireframes Iteration

Week 3: High-Fidelity Design

  • UI Design & UI Kit Development
  • Cross-functional Team (XFN) Collaboration
  • Developer Handoff via Figma

Key Features

  • Seamless Authorization: Quick and secure login process for effortless access.

  • Dashboard Overview: A centralized hub for policy details, upcoming payments, and alerts.

  • Policy Renewal & Upgrade: Easy-to-use workflows for renewing or enhancing policies.

  • Document Management: Download policy documents with a single click.

  • Dynamic Notifications: Personalized alerts for payment reminders and policy expirations.

  • Arabic Localization: Full support for bilingual (English & Arabic) users, respecting regional preferences.

Results & Impact (Q3 2024)

The project delivered significant improvements:

  • 50% Reduction in Branch Visits: Minimized in-person dependency.
  • 25% Increase in Policy Sales: Enhanced convenience boosted customer engagement.
  • 30% Faster Application Processing: Streamlined workflows accelerated service delivery.

Reflection

I carefully considered bilingual fluidity, family-centric user behavior, and the local context to create a seamless and accessible interface. The success of the Insurance Policy Management interface highlighted how digital transformation can enhance user satisfaction, improve business outcomes, and position a brand as a thought leader in its industry.
