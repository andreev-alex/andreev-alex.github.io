Empowering Users to Manage Their Finances

Societe Generale Mobile Banking

As part of Societe Generale’s digital transformation initiative, I contributed to designing several key features for their mobile banking app, which serves 350,000 individual clients in Eastern Europe. The app provides essential financial services, including money transfers, payments, bank statement orders, and loyalty programs. My role focused on enhancing usability and aligning the Android design with the iOS version to deliver a seamless and intuitive user experience.

About Societe Generale

Societe Generale is one of Europe’s largest financial conglomerates, serving millions of individual and corporate clients globally. Known for its innovative financial solutions, the bank is at the forefront of digital transformation in the financial industry.

Users Problem

Users faced several challenges with the mobile banking app:

  • Navigation and workflows were unintuitive, making simple tasks cumbersome.
  • The Android version lacked visual and functional alignment, leading to fragmented user experiences.
  • High customer support requests indicated difficulty in completing routine tasks independently.

Solution

I designed several key features for the Android version of the app, focusing on usability, consistency, and scalability. My contributions addressed critical pain points and ensured visual alignment with the iOS version. Core solutions included:

  • Designing workflows for money transfers, payments, and loyalty programs.
  • Building reusable UI components for a scalable design system.
  • Conducted testing to validate designs, iterating based on user feedback.
  • Worked closely with product managers and developers to align designs with technical constraints and business goals.

Key Features

  • Money Transfers & Payments: Developed streamlined interfaces for completing transactions quickly and confidently.

  • Rewards Program Management: Enabled users to track and redeem rewards within the app.

  • Design System Integration: Created reusable UI components to ensure consistency and accelerate future updates.

Results & Impact

Designed features delivered measurable success:

  • 30% Increase in Transaction Frequency: Simplified workflows encouraged users to engage more frequently.
  • 25% Decrease in Customer Support Requests: Improved usability empowered users to complete tasks independently.
  • 90% Visual Consistency Across Platforms: Unified Android and iOS experiences strengthened brand trust.

Reflection

By focusing on intuitive workflows and cross-platform consistency, I helped transform a complex banking app into a tool that users relied on daily. Moving forward, I’d explore integrating predictive analytics to provide personalized financial insights and further enhance user engagement.
