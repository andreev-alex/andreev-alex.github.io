Qatar Insurance Journal

This project aimed to engage users with captivating content while seamlessly integrating with the qic.online website to drive insurance purchases. As the Lead Product Designer, I led the development of a vibrant web interface for a social media blog focusing on insurance insights and the rich lifestyle of Qatar.

Key features

Diverse Article Topics

The interface offers a diverse range of articles covering various topics, including 'Cars & Bikes', 'Insurance Basics','Exploring Qatar', etc. This curated content enriches users with valuable insights while catering to their diverse interests

Key features

Category Filtering

Users can easily filter articles based on specific categories, allowing for a personalized browsing experience tailored to individual preferences and interests

Key features

Author Exploration

The interface enables users to explore content from different authors, fostering connections and offering a platform for diverse voices within the insurance and Qatar lifestyle community

Key features

Simplified Insurance Terms

Complex insurance terms are demystified and presented in an understandable manner, empowering users to grasp essential concepts and make informed decisions about insurance coverage

Key features

Newsletter Subscription

Users have the option to subscribe to a newsletter, staying updated with the latest articles, news, and insights directly in their inbox, fostering ongoing engagement and brand loyalty

Key features

Search

The interface includes a robust search feature, allowing users to find every article, term, or author throughout the entire blog. This enhances accessibility and enables users to quickly locate relevant content based on their specific interests or queries
