Federal Bank's Rewards Program

'Sber Spasibo' is a rewards program in Eastern Europe that caters to an active user base of 73 million customers. They can save money on everyday purchases by using this rewards program.

Business Problem

Decreasing Customer Engagement

Clients couldn’t use the rewards program directly on their SBER devices. When they wanted to use the rewards program, they had to navigate through WebView to access the website. This inconvenience led to decreased customer engagement.

Business Goal

Engage and Retain Clients in the Program

  1. Encourage customers to use goods and services provided by the program's partners.
  2. Promote the usage of the bank's products, mainly debit and credit card transactions.
Task & Individual Contribution

Design a Cross-Platform Solution

As a Senior Product Designer on this project, my primary focus was on developing a cross-platform design that seamlessly integrated across various platforms, including iOS, tablet, and Smart TV. This allowed Sberbank customers to fully interact with the "Sber Spasibo" rewards program without leaving these applications.

User Flow

The user flow for the whole product feature consists of four large sections.

iOS

Entry Point

Users can access the rewards program through a banner on the home page of the iOS app.

iOS

Try it on Your Smartphone, or Explore the Design Below

Point your camera towards the code to open the Figma interactive prototype on your phone.

iOS

Promo Page

The promo page highlights the benefits of the rewards program and guides users on how to earn and redeem points.

iOS

Registration

Users can register using only their date of birth and phone number

iOS

Partners

It's possible to view Sberbank's different partners with specific conditions for each. Due to the rewards program's business constraints, some partners may be separated into groups where users can only spend or accumulate bonuses

iOS

Rewards Home Page

On the main screen of the rewards program, customers can view the total amount of bonuses available for spending. Additionally, they can see their current rewards program level displayed below that amount. Furthermore, they can explore services that allow them to redeem a portion of their purchases in the form of bonuses

Rewards Grades

There are four levels of the rewards program 'Sber Spasibo', each of which has its own set of benefits

iOS

Rewards Grades

Users can access more specific information about their current grade level by tapping on it

iOS

Rewards Objectives

If a user wants to qualify for a higher rewards program level, they need to complete objectives for one quarter. On this screen, they can view specific information about each task

iOS

Rewards Categories

Users can receive more bonuses on specific purchases by choosing additional categories

iOS

FAQ

Covers most use cases, to reduce the need for support
