About

«Sber Spasibo» is a rewards program for Sberbank clients. Its members can pay for goods and services (up to 99% of the purchase) usingthe rewards.

According to Brand Finance, Sberbank is the strongest banking brand in the world. The company is on its way to becoming one of the world's best fintech and technology companies. Sberbank has 101 million active individual clients

Entry point & promo page

On the «SberSalut» application's main page, placed a bannerto go to the Sber Spasibo program. After tapping on the banner,we get to the promo page of the program, where we describe advantages and let the client connect to it

Partners

On the partner page, the client can see which companies, in cooperation with Sberbank, can accrue or accept bonuses as compensation and how much. These categories are divided into tabs «All», «Accumulate», «Spend» on the main screen.

You can select a specific partner and view the conditions for accruingor debiting bonuses and go to their corporate website

Registration

By clicking on the «Join the program» button on the promo page, we get to the registration screen, where we enter the minimumset of data - phone number and date of birth. You can also readthe program's rules at the bottom of the form.

After entering the data, we see a notification of successful registration in the program and get the opportunity to accumulate or spend bonuses and view information on them

Home page

On the main screen, the client sees the current number of bonuses and can solve several tasks:

  • see your current privilege level (4 in total)
  • view your current privileges at your level
  • see what features are available at other levels and what is needed for this
  • view a list of tasks that must be completed to move to a higher level

Privilege grades

The program has four grades. The higher the grade of the client, the more rewards he can accumulate. The grade is higher if you use Sberbank cards and services more often

Privilege tasks

Each privilege grade has its own set of tasks that the client needsto complete within three months

Categories

The client can receive more bonuses for the categories of goods he needs, which he can choose in the application

FAQ

Clients frequently use the FAQ screen. Here they find a large number of explanations for different situations
